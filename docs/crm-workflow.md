# CRM Workflow

1. Employee creates lead manually or imports Excel.
2. System checks duplicate phone/email.
3. If duplicate exists, import/form shows “Duplicate contact found”.
4. Owner/Manager can override or reassign.
5. Employee contacts client by call, WhatsApp or email.
6. Call requires manual log and mandatory recording upload.
7. WhatsApp opens employee’s own WhatsApp with prefilled message.
8. Email opens CRM compose modal and sends from employee Hostinger email.
9. Client email replies sync into CRM using IMAP.
10. Follow-up date creates dashboard reminder.
11. Deal moves across pipeline stages until converted/lost.
12. Owner/Manager uses reports for performance and pipeline.
